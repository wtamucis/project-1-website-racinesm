Welcome to Momentum Mobile Physical Therapy

Momentum Mobile PT provides personalized physical therapy sessions delivered to your home or workplace. Our mission is to make recovery convenient, empowering, and effective — so you can move with confidence again.

Our licensed clinicians create treatment plans designed around your lifestyle. Whether you’re rehabbing from an injury, improving posture, or reducing daily pain, we meet you where you are — literally and figuratively — to help restore strength, balance, and mobility.

Every body is unique. That’s why our concierge-style approach focuses on individualized attention, evidence-based practice, and long-term wellness habits that keep you feeling your best.
